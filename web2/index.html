The Burmese

The Burmese, or Burmese Shorthair, is a breed of shorthair cats. The Burmese cat is distinguished by

  • a muscular,
  • strong body,
  • short shiny coat,
  • large rounded yellow eyes.
Burmese are affectionate, playful, attached to humans, tolerant of being around other cats, as well as dogs.
